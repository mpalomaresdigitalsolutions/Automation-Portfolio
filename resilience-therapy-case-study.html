Section 02

What Was Done

A breakdown of the Google Ads optimizations implemented to deliver better reach and more booked appointments.

01
🎯 Precision Audience Targeting
Refined geographic and demographic targeting to reach people actively searching for mental health therapy services in the right locations — eliminating irrelevant impressions and wasted spend.
02
✍️ Ad Copy Optimization
Rewrote ad copy to speak directly to people seeking therapy support — empathetic messaging that resonates with the audience's intent, improving click-through rate and quality score.
03
🔍 Keyword Strategy
Built a focused keyword list targeting high-intent therapy-related searches, paired with a strong negative keyword list to block unqualified traffic and reduce cost-per-click.
04
📊 Conversion Tracking Setup
Implemented proper conversion tracking via Google Tag Manager to capture appointment bookings and contact form submissions — giving clear visibility into what ads were driving real clients.
05
🖥️ Landing Page Alignment
Aligned ad messaging with landing page content to improve Quality Score and conversion rate — making it seamless for a potential client to go from ad click to booking confirmation.
06
⚙️ Ongoing Optimization
Weekly performance reviews with continuous bid adjustments, ad testing, and campaign refinements to ensure a steady and growing stream of new client inquiries and bookings.
