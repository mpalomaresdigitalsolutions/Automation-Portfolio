Most clients see improved lead flow within 2-3 weeks. Full optimization (Quality Score improvements, bid refinement, audience targeting) takes 4-6 weeks for maximum impact.

Absolutely. Month-to-month agreements — no long-term contracts. If you're not seeing value, you can cancel at any time. I'm confident the results will speak for themselves.
